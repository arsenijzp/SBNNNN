WHAT IS SBN?

The People's Security Service (SBN) is an elite paramilitary unit created to protect civil liberties. We do not answer to corrupt officials and act in the interests of the people. Our goal: eliminating threats, protecting human rights, clearing illegal checkpoints, and conducting precision tactical operations of any complexity. We step in when the law stops working.

— RECRUITMENT

CONTRACT CONDITIONS

SBN values its operatives. We provide the best conditions for those willing to risk their lives for the freedom of others.

STABLE COMPENSATION

Salaries are paid strictly on time, without delays. Substantial bonuses are provided for successful raids, assaults, and high-value target eliminations.

ELITE EQUIPMENT

Full access to advanced tactical weaponry, heavy armor, and specialized transport that is unavailable to regular army units.

FULL IMMUNITY

SBN operatives act outside standard jurisdiction. Command provides full legal and physical protection for every soldier deployed in the field.
